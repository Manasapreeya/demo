  • Digital Transformation


    Since the term Digital Transformation has become so widely used and misunderstood in many areas, that it has become unhelpful.



  • What is Not a Digital Transformation :


    • X Digital Transformation is not only a million dollar investment
    • X Digital Transformation is not only upgrading legacy systems
    • X Digital Transformation is not only implementing Agile methodology

  • What Is a Digital Transformation :


    Digital Transformation doesn't fit into any single definition. An ideal Digital Transformation for any given organization would utilize advanced technologies, implement speed to market process, data driven, etc. The ultimate outcomes should enhance or entirely change the customer experiences and should solve customer problems by continuous business and technology innovations.

    As part of Digital Transformation, strategically adopting digital technologies to improve processes, productivity, deliver value & controlling cost are most important.
